# Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

## Nurse Prep by Candas

Nurse Prep by Candas, shown on some devices as Nurse Prep, is an educational application developed by Candas Feighert to support nursing students. This app does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

## Information We Collect

We do not collect, sell, or share personal information automatically through this app.

The app does not require account creation and does not request sensitive personal information for core use.

## Data Storage

The app may store learning progress and preferences locally on your device to improve your experience. This data stays on your device unless your device backup settings copy app data to your own cloud backup.

## Data Retention

Because this app stores progress and preferences locally on your device, that data remains on your device until you delete the app or clear the app's storage through your device settings.

If you enable device or cloud backup features, locally stored app data may also be included in your personal backup and kept according to your device provider or cloud backup provider's retention practices.

We do not maintain user accounts for this app and we do not keep your study progress on our own servers.

## How to Delete Your Data

You can delete locally stored app data at any time by:

- uninstalling the app from your device, or
- clearing the app's storage/data in your device settings.

Because this app does not maintain user accounts or store your study progress on our own servers, uninstalling the app or clearing app storage is the primary way to remove your app data.

If you contact us by email, you may also request deletion of any support communications you have sent us.

## Third-Party Services

At this time, the app does not use third-party advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or payment SDKs inside the app.

The app does not use third-party services inside the app to collect personal information from users.

## Support Communications

If you contact us for support by email, we may keep that communication for a reasonable period of time to respond to your request, troubleshoot issues, keep basic support records, and comply with legal obligations if required.

Support emails are not used for advertising or sold to third parties.

## Children

This app is intended for general educational use and is not directed to children under 13.

## Security

We take reasonable measures to keep the app secure. No method of electronic storage or transmission is 100 percent secure.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
