# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

## Candas Feighert RN Prep

Candas Feighert RN Prep is an educational application designed to support nursing students. This app does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

## Information We Collect

We do not collect, sell, or share personal information through this app.

The app does not require account creation and does not request sensitive personal information for core use.

## Data Storage

The app may store learning progress and preferences locally on your device to improve your experience. This data stays on your device unless your device backup settings copy app data to your own cloud backup.

## Third-Party Services

At this time, the app does not use third-party advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or payment SDKs inside the app.

## Children

This app is intended for general educational use and is not directed to children under 13.

## Security

We take reasonable measures to keep the app secure. No method of electronic storage or transmission is 100 percent secure.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
